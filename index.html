What are the types of two-wheeler insurance in India?

Third-Party Liability-Only Cover

In India, the Motor Vehicles Act specifies that a bike should be insured for any liability that might arise due to any loss or damage suffered by third parties in an accident caused by the insured bike. This type of bike insurance plan is popularly called a third-party bike insurance. Under this policy, if the insured bike is responsible for causing an accident, then any damage to the third-party vehicle and injury or loss of life of the third party shall be reimbursed.

Standalone Own Damage Cover

An Standalone Own Damage Two-Wheeler Insurance policy is a type of bike insurance policy that covers the damages caused to the insured two-wheeler. It covers damages caused by natural and man-made calamities, theft of the bike and total damage of the bike (damaged beyond any repairs). However, this policy does not cover any third-party liabilities. As a result, this policy can only be bought together with the legally mandatory third-party liability cover.

Comprehensive Bike Insurance

This policy, as the name suggests, offers comprehensive 360-degree protection not only to your bike but for you as well as against any third-party liabilities. A Comprehensive Bike Insurance is a type of two wheeler insurance that offers an own-damage cover for the insured bike, coverage against any legal and financial third-party liabilities and personal accident cover to the owner-driver. Additionally, you can get targeted coverage against specific circumstances by including a list of unique add-ons with your policy. The popular add-ons are Zero Depreciation Cover, Roadside Assistance Cover and so on.
